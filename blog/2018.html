Blog - Looking back at 2018

<< Back to Blogs

  • 24 Dec 2018

  • Mike

It is the end of 2018 and it has been a great year for the Glass.Mapper.Sc project.

I would just like to say a massive thank you to everyone who has supported the project in 2018, especially the Rockstars who are supporting the project via Patreon!!

Some of the highlights of 2018 were:

  • Release of Version 5 of the project. V5 cleaned up a lot of code in the project by removing unpopular features and refocusing the project on it's core aim of mapping models.
  • Launch of the online training course. The training course takes you through all the key features of the framework. Those who complete it get a certificate of completion.
  • Sitecore 9.1 support. 
  • Started to make the project sustainable via the Patreon Supports page.

Next year, with the support of the Glass community, I hope that we can continue to grow and improve the framework. 

Again I want to thank everyone who has supported the Glass.Mapper.Sc project and I will definitely be raising a Glass to you all of you on New Years eve!

 Mike

Founder

I have been working with Sitecore for the last 10 years, working on a variety of clients in the commercial, charity and government sectors.  I have a passion for programming and love to develop tools that help other developers deliver incredible solutions.

About 3 years ago I started the Glass.Mapper (then called Glass.Sitecore.Mapper) project. The first release was designed specifically for Sitecore but it soon became obvious that the power of Glass.Mapper could be extended to other content management systems. The support and uptake from the community has been amazing and it has been great to hear the feedback.

I have presented at variety of different events such as the SUGCON 2017, SUGCON 2016 and various Sitecore User Groups. I have also been awarded Sitecore MVP status since 2011.

You can follow me on twitter @mikeedwards83.





Glass is home to the #1 ORM for Sitecore. Glass.Mapper is the easiest way to map data from your Sitecore solution to your code.

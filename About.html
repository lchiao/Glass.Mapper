About

I started working with Sitecore in 2008 and founded the Glass.Mapper.Sc open source project in 2011 with the aim of creating an ORM that felt and acted more like the ORMs I had used when working with SQL. to supply support and training for the Glass.Mapper Open Source ORM in Sitecore, as well as supplying a wide range of software development and consultancy services. Mike has been a Sitecore MVP since 2011 and has 10 years of development experience, 8 of those with the Sitecore CMS. 

We aim to develop software solutions that are innovative, interesting and challenging and deliver on our customers needs.
